Who Am I?

Hi, thanks for visiting my website! I'm Denise and I've made this space on the web as I want to document my career change from content marketer and SEO specialist to software engineer. It's going to be a marathon, rather than a sprint, so I hope this space will help me to reflect on the experience and remember it as my career progresses.

My Blog

I've created a blog where I will track my experiences, open discussions and bounce ideas around for peers and mentors. I hope you find my posts interesting. Come on in!
